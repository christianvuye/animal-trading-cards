Lion

Lions are the only cats that live in groups, which are called prides. A pride can consist of up to 30 lions.

  • Habitat: Savannahs and grasslands
  • Diet: Carnivorous, feeding mainly on large herbivores
  • Average Lifespan: 10-14 years in the wild
  • Scientific Name: Panthera leo

Lions are known as the "king of the jungle" due to their majestic appearance and position as top predators in their habitat. They are social animals and play an important role in maintaining the ecological balance of their environment.
